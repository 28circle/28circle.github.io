Welcome! ようこそ！

28*Circle is a bilingual (English and Japanese) activity group/circle located in Toronto. Our main activity is our weekly board game meetup! Want to know more? Were you looking for our Instagram? Or maybe you just want to contact us?

28(にエイト)サークルはトロントで毎週行われています。英語と日本語で会話をしながらボードゲームをするサークルです。
